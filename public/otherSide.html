Local Attractions

Urumqi Zoo

During a visit to Urumqi a visit to the zoo is a must. The place is an a attraction for children as well elders when traveling together. Urmuqi zoo is a great place for goups.


People's Theater of Xinjiang Uygur

Relic of the Soviet days, this wonderful multiplex cinema has recently been renovated. You enter the main hall where massive chandeliers drip from the ceiling. There are a whole range of halls from the massive main one to a whole variety of little bijou halls.

Local Hiking & Biking Trails

Tianshan Mountains in Urumqi

Located just 60 km or so from Urumqi, it's the most accessible piece of nature from the city. You can take a tourist bus and visit the grand canyon (the attendant also sings some karaoke for you. Breathtaking views of the mountains and a spectacular lake.


Red Hill in Urumqi

The RED HILL Park is centrally located and easily reached by public transports. It is near the SOUTHERN AIRLINES PEARL HOTEL. Many shops and restaurants around the Park. It is a good and quite tiring climb to the top; whereby one gets a good view of the surroundings
